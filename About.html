About Online Election Services

Fuctions
This website is an online election system that uses digital platforms and technologies to conduct voting processes, enabling eligible individuals to cast their votes remotely through the internet. This method offers an alternative to traditional in-person voting systems.

The aims of an online voting system encompass several key objectives aimed at improving the electoral process, enhancing accessibility, and ensuring the integrity of the voting system. Aims includes;Accessibility,Convenience,Increased Voter Participation,etc.
